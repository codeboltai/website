Key Insights from the Demo

🐜 Self-Organization

No central coordinator tells agents what to do. They sense pheromone signals and self-organize around important work.

💬 Agent Deliberation

Agents discuss and vote on decisions. See how they reach consensus on architectural approaches without human intervention.

⚡ Parallel Execution

Multiple agents work simultaneously on different tasks. True concurrency, not sequential turn-taking.
