Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? +

Our free trial gives you full access to the Developer plan for 14 days. You'll have access to all core features including up to 10 concurrent agents, basic stigmergy coordination, and persistent memory. No credit card required to start your trial.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle. We prorate charges when upgrading mid-cycle.

What counts as an "agent"? +

An agent is an AI entity that performs development tasks. This includes coding agents, testing agents, documentation agents, and any other specialized agents you create. The concurrent agent limit refers to how many agents can be actively working at the same time.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions? +

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team to discuss eligibility and pricing options.

Is there a commitment period for the Enterprise plan? +

Enterprise plans typically involve an annual commitment to ensure service stability and priority support. However, we offer flexible terms based on your organization's needs. Contact our sales team for customized arrangements.
