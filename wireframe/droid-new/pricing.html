Frequently Asked Questions

How is Codebolt different from GitHub Copilot or Cursor?

Copilot and Cursor are single-agent tools that provide incremental (1.2x) productivity improvements. Codebolt is a multi-agent platform that delivers 5-10x productivity through parallelization. Instead of one AI assistant, you deploy swarms of agents working simultaneously on different tasks. We operate at a completely different level—orchestration, not just assistance.

What's the difference between Pro and Team tiers?

Pro is for individual developers who need full access to Codebolt's features. Team adds collaboration features: shared swarms, team-wide agent sharing, role-based access control, and team analytics. Team also includes higher agent limits (50+ vs 10) and priority support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll have immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and you'll retain data access according to your new plan's retention policy.

Do you offer discounts for open source or education?

Yes! We offer free Pro licenses for qualified open source projects and 50% discounts for students and educators. Contact us at community@codebolt.dev for more information.

What happens to my agents and data if I cancel?

Your agents and data remain accessible according to your plan's retention policy. Free tier users have 30-day memory retention. Paid plans have unlimited retention. If you downgrade from paid to free, old data beyond 30 days will be archived but can be exported upon request.

Is Enterprise self-hosting really fully self-contained?

Yes. Enterprise self-hosted deployments run entirely on your infrastructure with no external dependencies. Your code never leaves your network. We provide complete installation guides, Docker containers, and support for deployment on your own servers or private cloud.

How do concurrent agents work?

Concurrent agents are agents actively running at the same time. You can have more agents configured than your concurrent limit—they just won't all run simultaneously. When one finishes, another starts automatically. This lets you manage larger agent pools efficiently.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) for paid plans. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice, wire transfer, or ACH. Annual billing is available for all paid plans with a 20% discount.
