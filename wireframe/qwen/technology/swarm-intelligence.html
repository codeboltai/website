Swarm Intelligence

Collective Intelligence at Scale BREAKTHROUGH

The world's first implementation of true swarm intelligence in software development. 100+ AI agents working in parallel with collective problem-solving capabilities that exceed individual agent intelligence. Distributed intelligence without central control.

Unlike traditional AI systems that rely on single agents or simple parallelization, Codebolt's swarm intelligence emerges from the interaction of multiple specialized agents following simple rules. Through stigmergy-based coordination and externalized memory, the swarm exhibits complex collective behaviors that solve problems beyond individual agent capabilities. Published Research: "Emergent Intelligence in Stigmergic Swarms: From Simple Rules to Complex Collective Behaviors" (IJCAI 2026).
