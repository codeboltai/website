Enterprise Architect

VP of Engineering / Principal Architect at 500+ Developer Companies

The Scenario

Alex leads engineering at a Fortune 500 company with 500+ developers across multiple teams. The board wants to double development output without doubling headcount. Current AI coding tools help individual developers but don't scale—they hit context limits on the massive codebase, provide no audit trails for compliance, and can't coordinate work across teams.

Alex needs a solution that can scale capacity enterprise-wide while maintaining security, compliance, and visibility requirements that the security team demands.

Solution with Codebolt

Codebolt enables Alex to deploy hundreds of AI agents across the organization's codebases. Each team gets their own agent swarms that understand their specific domain, remember past decisions, and coordinate with other agents working on related systems.

  • Enterprise-scale deployment: Run 100+ agents simultaneously across multiple repositories and teams
  • Full audit trails: Every agent action logged for compliance—complete request/response history, tool calls, and decision rationale
  • Self-hosting option: Keep all code and agent interactions within your security perimeter
  • Knowledge persistence: Agents remember architectural decisions, coding standards, and team preferences across sessions
  • 24/7 operations: Development continues around the clock without burnout or overtime

Expected Outcomes

3x Development velocity increase
50% Reduction in time-to-market
100% Audit trail coverage
24/7 Development operations
