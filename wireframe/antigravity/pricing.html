Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to pay for each agent?

No. Our pricing is per-user, not per-agent. Run as many agents as your plan allows within the concurrent limit.

Can I use my own LLM API keys?

Yes. Bring your own API keys for OpenAI, Anthropic, or any supported provider. You pay your LLM costs directly.

What counts as a "concurrent agent"?

Agents that are actively running at the same time. Terminated agents don't count against your limit.

Can I self-host?

Enterprise plans include self-hosted deployment options. Contact sales for details.
