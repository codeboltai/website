The Core Insight

Build Smarter Environments,
Not Smarter Agents

Traditional AI tools focus on making individual agents smarter. We focus on making the environment intelligent—so simple agents can work as sophisticated societies.

Like ant colonies that build complex structures through pheromone trails, or markets that coordinate millions through price signals—coordination emerges from the environment itself.
