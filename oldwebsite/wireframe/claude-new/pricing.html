Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

Copilot and Cursor are excellent single-agent tools that improve individual productivity by 20-30%. Codebolt operates at a different level—we enable you to run 50-100+ agents in parallel, working 24/7. While Copilot helps one developer write code faster, Codebolt lets you conduct an entire AI orchestra. Think of it this way: Copilot is a very smart intern. Codebolt is an entire engineering team.

Yes! Self-hosting is available for Enterprise customers. This means your code never leaves your infrastructure—important for companies with strict security, compliance, or IP requirements. Contact our sales team to learn more about self-hosted deployment options.

Codebolt supports multiple AI models including GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and local models. You can even use different models for different agents—your testing agent might use one model while your coding agent uses another. This gives you flexibility and best-of-breed performance for every task.

Yes! Our Community tier is free forever with up to 3 concurrent agents. For Pro and Team plans, we offer 14-day free trials so you can experience the full power of multi-agent development before committing.

Concurrent agents are the number of agents actively running at the same time. You can create and save unlimited agent configurations, but your plan limits how many can execute simultaneously. For example, with Pro (10 agents), you could run 5 feature agents, 2 testing agents, 1 documentation agent, and 2 security agents all at once.

If you need more concurrent agents than your plan allows, additional agents will queue until capacity becomes available. You can upgrade your plan at any time to increase your agent capacity, or contact us about Enterprise options for unlimited scaling.

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. We believe in flexibility—no long-term contracts required.

Yes! We offer special pricing for open source projects, educational institutions, and non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team to learn more about these programs.
