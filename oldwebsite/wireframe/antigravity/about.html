About Us

Building the Future of
Software Development

Mission

Design Smarter Environments

We're not building smarter agents—we're designing smarter environments where simple agents can work as sophisticated societies. Like ant colonies, markets, and institutions, complex behavior emerges from simple local rules and shared environmental signals.

Philosophy

Our Principles

🐜 Stigmergy Over Orchestration

Decentralized coordination through environmental signals scales better than central control.

💾 Environment-First Memory

Memory belongs to the environment, not agents. Knowledge persists across generations.

👥 Humans as Participants

Humans participate in the swarm with the same tools as agents—not as external observers.

🔍 No Black Boxes

Full observability into every decision. You can always understand what happened and why.

⚖️ Emergent Governance

Policies evolve from collective experience. The swarm develops its own culture and norms.

📈 Linear Scaling

Coordination overhead should scale linearly, not quadratically. 200+ agents should just work.
