Job Coordination System

Coordinate Work Across
Hundreds of Agents

A complete job management system designed for multi-agent coordination. Locking, bidding, blocking, and split proposals ensure smooth parallel execution.

Job Structure

Job Management

Jobs with unique IDs, types (bug, feature, task, epic, chore), status tracking, priority levels, and dependency management.

Race Prevention

Job Locking

Agents acquire locks when starting work. Prevents multiple agents from overwriting each other. Unlock request system for polite resource sharing.

Fair Distribution

Job Bidding

Agents bid on jobs with reasons and priority scores. Fair work distribution based on capability and availability.

Task Decomposition

Split Proposals

Agents can propose job splits. Multiple proposals compete. Deliberation selects best approach. Tracks discovery lineage.

Dependency Tracking

Job Blockers

Track what prevents job execution. Link to blocking jobs. Resolution tracking. Agents can add and resolve blockers.

Organization

Job Groups

Container for related jobs. Hierarchical parent-child relationships. Auto-incrementing sequence numbers.

Feature Category
Stigmergy-Based Coordination

Self-Organizing Intelligence
Through Pheromone Signals

Indirect coordination through environmental signals, inspired by ant colonies. No central orchestrator needed—agents respond to signals left by others.

[Diagram: Pheromone Signal Flow Between Agents]

Pheromone Types

Pheromone Purpose Decay
request_split Signal that a job should be split into smaller tasks None
importance Signal job priority to attract agent attention None
saturation Signal that a job is heavily worked on Slow (0.05/hr)
takeup_interest Signal interest in taking a job Moderate (0.1/hr)
task_not_ready Signal blocking dependencies None
job_available Signal that blockers have been resolved Slow (0.05/hr)
files_blocked Signal that files are reserved by intent None
