Context Factors for Selection

The adaptive system considers multiple factors when selecting coordination mechanisms.

Swarm Load

Higher load triggers lock-based coordination for faster allocation and reduced overhead.

Threshold: >80% utilization
Task Complexity

Complex tasks benefit from market-based coordination with expertise matching.

Threshold: >7 complexity score
Agent Diversity

Homogeneous agents use pheromone-based coordination for better load balancing.

Threshold: <30% diversity index
Resource Constraints

Limited resources trigger lock-based coordination to prevent conflicts.

Threshold: <10% available
Time Sensitivity

Urgent tasks use lock-based coordination for fastest allocation.

Threshold: <1 hour deadline
Team Structure

Formal roles and responsibilities trigger social signaling coordination.

Threshold: Role-based tasks
